Weathering with You is a 2019 animated film about a runaway teenager named Hodaka who meets and befriends Hina, a girl with the supernatural ability to control the weather. They start a business using her powers to bring sunshine to a perpetually rainy Tokyo, but their actions have unforeseen consequences. The story is a romantic fantasy that explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the relationship between humanity and nature
Tsuneo is a university student, and Josee is a young girl who has rarely gone out of the house by herself due to her being unable to walk. The two meet when Tsuneo finds Josee's grandmother taking her out for a morning walk.
A Silent Voice is a Japanese animated film about a former bully, Shoya Ishida, who seeks to atone for his past actions by reconnecting with Shoko Nishimiya, a deaf girl he used to bully. After Shoko transfers schools, Shoya becomes an outcast himself and, burdened by guilt and regret, attempts to befriend her as a high schooler to make amends. The story explores themes of bullying, disability, depression, anxiety, suicide, forgiveness, and redemption through their complex relationship
Ocean Waves is a Studio Ghibli coming-of-age story about a love triangle between two high school best friends, Taku and Yutaka, and a moody transfer student from Tokyo named Rikako. Set in the Japanese city of Kochi, the film follows Taku as he reflects on his senior year of high school after encountering Rikako, exploring themes of friendship, first love, and the emotional complexities of growing up.
